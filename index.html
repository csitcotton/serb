About The Workshop

Welcome to the "AI in Healthcare Workshop",sponsored by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB), a statutory body under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. Established in 2009 through an Act of the Parliament of India, SERB is committed to promoting and supporting cutting-edge research and innovation. This exclusive workshop is designed specifically for faculty members of colleges, providing a platform to explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in the field of healthcare. Through engaging sessions, interactive discussions, participants will gain valuable insights into the latest advancements, applications, and ethical considerations in leveraging AI to enhance healthcare delivery. Join us to unlock new horizons in healthcare innovation.
